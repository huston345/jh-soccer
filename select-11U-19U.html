Select Program (Ages 11-19)

Welcome to our Select program, part of the prestigious Iowa United Soccer Club. Our select program is dedicated to developing elite soccer players through advanced training and competitive play. We focus on enhancing technical skills, tactical understanding, and physical fitness, ensuring each player reaches their full potential. Our players compete in top-tier leagues and tournaments, representing the highest standards of excellence and sportsmanship. Join us to experience elite soccer development in a supportive and challenging environment.

Program Details

  • Age Groups: 11U - 19U
  • Location: Various fields across the Des Moines Metro area
  • Schedule: Weekdays and weekends, depending on league and tournament schedules
  • Equipment: Full training kit including home and away jerseys, shinguards, soccer cleats, and soccer ball
  • Cost: $350 per season or $650 for the entire year

Training Schedule

Players will train 3-4 times a week, each session lasting approximately 90 minutes. Our intensive training schedule is designed to provide players with the skills and fitness necessary to excel at the highest levels of competition.

Coaching Staff & Team Formation

Our select teams are coached by licensed professionals who are dedicated to fostering individual player development. Teams are formed based on skill level, ensuring competitive balance and maximum growth for each player.

League Games & Tournaments

Select teams participate in the Iowa Soccer League and various prestigious tournaments throughout the year. Our focus is on competitive success while maintaining the highest standards of sportsmanship and team spirit.

Join Our Elite Program

If you are ready to take your game to the next level, we invite you to join our Select program. Experience the benefits of elite soccer development and become part of the Iowa United Soccer Club legacy.
