Who could you become?

J-Hawk Soccer Academy helps boys and girls improve their skills, build self-confidence, make new friends and be part of a team. We provide authentic and creative year-round soccer coaching programs for all players and abilities.

The City Play model

Our courses have been designed to support the development of all players, while experiencing the J-Hawk training and playing philosophy. Each phase of the City Play model encourages young players to build on what they have learnt and develop their skills playing beautiful football.

All our sessions are delivered by coaches recruited and trained by J-Hawk, following the same philosophy used to develop City's own players, in a fun, positive, and safe learning environment.

 Our Young Player Development Classes aim to develop players at the start of their football journey, with weekly sessions focused on the enjoyment of playing, gaining confidence in their own ability, and inspiring a real passion for the game.

Our Young Player Development Program consists in bi-weekly training and game sessions focused on learning the fundamentals of attacking and defending, developing confidence and creativity in 1v1 situations, improving players' understanding of their role in a team, and learning how City's first team players compete at the highest level.
