Empowering Youth Through Sport

Player Pathway

Nurturing Young Soccer Enthusiasts at Every Stage

Ages 4-8: Foundation Phase

Focus: Introduction to soccer and developing a love for the game.

  • Respect for the badge
  • Teamwork
  • Foundational footwork
  • Basic decision making

Ages 9-10: Decision Phase

Focus: Choosing between Recreational Soccer and Academy Program.

  • Recreational Soccer: Entry-level or casual soccer experience, suitable for beginners or those seeking a less competitive environment.
  • Academy Program: Comprehensive, skill-oriented coaching designed to bridge the transition from recreational to more competitive play.

Ages 11-19: Development and Specialization

Focus: Tailored pathways for different levels of competition and development.

  • Recreational Pathway: Casual training emphasizing enjoyment of the game and fostering a fun atmosphere.
  • Select Pathway: Intensive training, competitive matches, and focused preparation for elite levels of play.

Invitation: Become part of the J-Hawk Soccer Club and embark on an exciting soccer journey that fosters athletic and personal growth.
