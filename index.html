Player Pathway

Nurturing Young Soccer Enthusiasts at Every Stage

Ages 4-8: Foundation Phase

Focus: Introduction to soccer and developing a love for the game.

  • Respect for the badge
  • Teamwork
  • Enjoyment of the outdoors

Ages 9-10: Decision Phase

Focus: Choosing between Recreational Soccer and Academy Program.

  • Recreational Soccer: Comfortable, relaxed environment.
  • Academy Program: Structured, skill-focused training.

Ages 11-19: Development and Specialization

Focus: Tailored pathways for different levels of competition and development.

  • Recreational Pathway: Fun and relaxed soccer experience.
  • Select Pathway: Competitive training, matches, and preparation for higher levels.
