Recreational 9U - 16U Program

Welcome to our 9-16U recreational program, a great way for players to be active, play with friends in a relaxed environment, and continue a lifelong love for the game of soccer.

9-16U Coed & Girls

This program is devoted to ensuring that kids have an opportunity to enjoy and play the game of soccer. Players will have the opportunity to develop individual skills and learn to play in a relaxed atmosphere with friends.

Details

  • Location: Martin Field (home games) and surrounding Des Moines Metro locations
  • Schedule: Saturdays or Sundays - Games scheduled by Iowa Soccer
  • Equipment: Two J-Hawk Jerseys with numbers (one navy, one white), Shinguards, Soccer Cleats, Soccer Ball, Water Bottle

For more information on how to register, please visit the registration section on our website or contact us directly.
