Academy Program (Ages 9-10)

Welcome to our Academy 9U - 10U program! This program bridges the gap between recreational and select soccer, introducing players to a more competitive level of play while fostering their individual growth.

Program Overview

The Academy 9U/10U program is designed to provide young athletes with a challenging yet supportive environment. Our goal is to enhance their technical skills, tactical understanding, and overall love for the game. The curriculum focuses on individual player development through balanced training sessions and competitive matches.

Is Your Child Ready for Academy?

Before enrolling in our Academy program, we encourage parents to consider the following questions:

Does your child have a strong understanding of soccer basics?

Can they effectively use both feet to dribble, pass, and shoot?

Are they physically fast and agile compared to their peers?

Do they show high levels of focus and concentration during practice and games?

Do they demonstrate sound technical skills such as control, dribbling, and shooting?

How well do they respond to coaching and guidance?

If your child has never played soccer before, we recommend starting with our recreational program. This allows them to build a solid foundation. If they showcase exceptional talent and skill, they may be invited to join the Academy program, or a waiver may be granted under special circumstances.

Program Details

Location: Martin Field (home games) and surrounding Des Moines Metro locations

Schedule: Saturdays and/or Sundays - all games are scheduled by Iowa Soccer

Equipment: Two Academy jerseys with numbers (one navy, one white), shinguards, soccer cleats, soccer ball, water bottle

Cost: $275 per season or $550 for the entire year

Training Schedule

Players will train 2-3 times a week at Townsend, each session lasting approximately 90 minutes. This consistent and intensive training schedule is designed to provide players with the skills and fitness necessary to excel at a higher level of competition.

Coaching Staff & Team Formation

Our Academy is coached by licensed professionals who are dedicated to fostering individual player development. We also encourage parent-volunteer coaches to take part in our training programs, including the 7v7 Grassroots course. Teams are formed to balance skill levels and ensure every player has the opportunity to grow.

League Games & Festivals

Academy teams participate in the Iowa Soccer Academy league, playing 7 scheduled league games each season. Our focus is on individual development, ensuring all players receive equal playing time and experience different positions.

Saturday Morning Games

Most games are scheduled for Saturday mornings, with some exceptions. Games are played in a 7v7 format, which provides ample ball contact for all players and encourages free movement across positions.
