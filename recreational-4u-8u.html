Recreational 4U - 8U Program

Our 4-8U recreational program is designed to introduce young children to the game of soccer in a fun and engaging environment. The focus is on developing basic skills and fostering a love for the game.

Program Details

  • Location: Various fields in the Des Moines Metro area
  • Schedule: Weekends - Games organized by J-Hawk Soccer Club
  • Equipment: J-Hawk jerseys, shinguards, soccer cleats, soccer ball, water bottle

Philosophy

Our program emphasizes fun, teamwork, and individual development. Coaches use age-appropriate activities to help children learn the fundamentals of soccer in a supportive and encouraging setting.

For more information on how to register, please visit the registration section on our website or contact us directly.
